| Purpose | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Ignition Red | Primary CTA, logo, keywords | #E8362E |

## Steel Blue — Cool Counterbalance
| Purpose | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Steel Blue | Secondary buttons, hover borders, accent lines | #3E5C76 |
| Steel Light | Subtle tints, secondary icon fills | #7C99B0 |

## Platinum — Replaces Chrome
| Purpose | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Platinum | Subtitles, descriptions, step labels | #9CA3AA |
| Graphite | Meta icons, least important labels | #686E73 |

## Key Design Rules
- Red = primary accent (CTAs, logo, keywords, activity CTA text/hover)
- Steel Blue = cool counterbalance (card hover borders, header/footer accent lines, icon bg)
- Platinum = neutral info (subtitles, descriptions, meta icons)
- Flare Coral = star ratings + Pro badge (brighter red, distinct from CTA red)
- Card hover border: Steel Blue30% (exploring = blue, clicking = red)
- Checkered flag: literal black/white or graphite
